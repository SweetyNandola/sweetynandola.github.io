About me

I’m Sweety Nandola, a passionate Data & BI Developer from Surat, India, specializing in database development, data visualization, and business intelligence solutions. With 4 years of hands-on experience working across healthcare, ERP, and service domains, I love transforming raw data into meaningful, intuitive insights that help businesses grow smarter.

My work focuses on making complex data simple, accurate, and accessible — whether through clean, optimized SQL databases, seamless ETL workflows, or interactive, easy-to-understand dashboards in Power BI, Looker Studio, and Tableau.

I believe that data should not just be stored — it should tell a story. That’s why I carefully craft each query, report, and dashboard to be both technically robust and visually insightful.

My aim is to help you see your data clearly, make faster, smarter decisions, and bring your business vision to life through actionable insights.

When I’m not buried in queries or dashboards, you’ll find me exploring new tech trends, enhancing my skills, or working on creative side projects.
